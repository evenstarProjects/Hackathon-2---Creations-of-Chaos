# HACKATHON 2 - CREATIONS OF CHAOS
My 2nd Hackathon with MadLab.

PROJECT BRIEF:
Digital Tools are something we all use in our day-to-day lives. It could be a calculator, a to-do list, social media application…
But for this Hackathon, we want to flip the script and make something truly chaotic! Over two days, we want you to create a digital tool in which nothing works quite as you expect. How you interpret this is entirely up to you!

# MY PLAN

### **The Digital Tool:**
To-Do List

### What is on the To-Do List:
1. Find the Encryption Key and decrypt the letters.
2. Solve The Hobbit riddles and type in the correct answer (make it timed?)
3. Sort/drag and drop the images into “chaos” and “not chaos” pairs (e.g. tv poster of Kaos goes into Chaos, tv poster of Jurassic Park goes into Not Chaos). The pairs will be in themes, e.g. a pair of films, a pair of games, a pair of books, a pair of paintings etc. where one is chaotic and one isn’t (e.g. a jackson pollock painting and a very angular OCD painting).
